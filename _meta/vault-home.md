## Structure
- **Clients:** `/work/clients/[name]/` — hub note, `/files`, `/meetings`, `/projects`. Scaffold with `/new-client`.
- **internal initiatives:** `/work/[company]/initiatives/[name]/` — strategic themes with sub-projects. Commerce, Health Platform, AI Strategy.
- **internal projects:** `/work/[company]/projects/[name]/` — standalone internal projects. FE, Hackathon, Quality Tool, and more.
- **People:** `/people/[company]/` — person pages by org. Recurring 1:1s get subfolders (e.g., `jordan-park/`, `chris-lang/`).
- **Domains:** `/domains/[name]/` — knowledge areas with hub note + `/files`. Scaffold with `/new-domain`.
- **Daily notes:** `/daily-notes/YYYY-MM-DD.md` — the daily working surface.
- **Intake:** `/intake/` — triage point for meetings, notes, clips. Clear with `/triage`.
- **Agent output:** `/agent-output/` — where Claude writes. Review and graduate.
- **Code:** `/code/` — symlinks to active code project docs (read-only).
