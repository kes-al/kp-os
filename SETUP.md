# KP/OS Setup

## Quick start

1. Open **Terminal** (press Cmd+Space, type "Terminal", press Enter)

2. Type `bash ` (with a space after it), then drag `setup.sh` from this folder into the Terminal window

3. Press Enter

4. Follow the prompts

That's it. The installer handles everything else.

## What gets installed

- **Obsidian** — where your notes live
- **Claude Code** — the AI that manages them
- **8 plugins** — pre-configured and ready to go
- **33 commands** — your daily workflow, capture, triage, discovery, and more

## After setup

Once the installer finishes, it'll tell you to open the Terminal panel inside Obsidian and run `/bootstrap`. That's a short interview (name, role, team, clients) that personalizes the system for you.

Then read `GUIDE.md` in your vault — it explains how everything works.
